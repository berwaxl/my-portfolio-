    HELLO

    A web developer is responsible for designing, building,
    and maintaining websites and web applications. Their
    work involves creating visually appealing and user-friendly
    interfaces using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, as
    well as developing the backend logic with technologies like
    PHP, Node.js, or Python.

    Web developers ensure that websites are responsive, fast,
    and secure across all devices. They often collaborate with
    designers and clients to bring ideas to life, optimize websites
    for performance and SEO, and handle deployments to make
    websites live and accessible to users.
